<h2>🧠 What is Arch Linux?</h2>

<p>
<strong>Arch Linux</strong> is a minimalist Linux distribution designed for users who want complete control over their system.
</p>

<p>
Instead of giving you a pre-configured setup, Arch provides a <strong>barebones base</strong> — you install and configure everything yourself.
</p>

<p>
This makes it one of the best choices if you want to <strong>learn Linux deeply</strong> and build a system tailored exactly to your needs.
</p>
